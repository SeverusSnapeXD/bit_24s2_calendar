How to Use Your Calendar

  1. Download Option: Click "Download ICS File" to save the calendar file to your device. You can then import it into your preferred calendar application.
  2. Subscribe Option: Click "Subscribe to Calendar" to get a link that you can add to your calendar app. This will automatically update when new events are added.
  3. Supported Apps: Works with Google Calendar, Apple Calendar, Outlook, and most other calendar applications.
  4. Automatic Updates: Subscribed calendars will automatically sync new events without manual downloads.
